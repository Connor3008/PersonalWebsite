About Me

Hi, I'm Connor Hege. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a concentration in Digital Arts from the University of North Carolina Wilmington, as well as an Associate's degree in Science from Cape Fear Community College.

I’m passionate about problem-solving through code and leveraging my expertise in programming languages like Java, C++, Python, and JavaScript. My experience spans developing efficient algorithms, building robust applications, and creating visually engaging digital experiences. Whether it's designing scalable systems, optimizing code performance, or exploring the intersection of technology and creativity, I thrive on tackling complex challenges.

My technical interests include algorithm design, object-oriented programming, and creating dynamic web applications. I also enjoy integrating technology with visual design to craft intuitive user experiences. Beyond coding, I stay curious about emerging trends in software development and continuously seek opportunities to enhance my skill set.
